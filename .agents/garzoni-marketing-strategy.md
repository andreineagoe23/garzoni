# Garzoni Marketing Strategy — Pre-Launch

_Last updated: 2026-04-08_
_Stage: Pre-launch, solo founder, no existing users_

---

## Positioning Statement

**For** self-directed adult learners in the UK and EU **who** want to build real, lasting financial literacy — not just read about it —

**Garzoni is** a gamified personal finance education platform

**that** combines structured learning paths, a 24/7 AI tutor, daily habit mechanics, and practical finance tools in one place,

**unlike** scattered YouTube content, passive finance apps, or one-off courses that don't stick.

---

## Primary ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

**Who:** Adults 22–45, UK-based (EU secondary), digitally native, financially curious but under-educated on money.

**They've tried:** YouTube finance channels, Reddit, maybe one Udemy course. Nothing stuck.

**They want:** To feel confident managing their money — not rich, not passive income — just clear-headed and in control.

**They respond to:** Structured progress, social proof from people like them, low commitment to start (free), daily habit hooks.

**Not ICP:** Someone who wants stock tips, investment advice, or a financial planning tool.

---

## Three Key Messages

### 1. Financial mastery is a craft — Garzoni is how you build it.

Finance isn't something you watch a video about and understand. It's a skill that develops over time with structured practice. Garzoni is built around that reality. Every lesson, tool, and daily habit moves you forward on a real path.

_Where to use:_ Brand story, about page, founder messaging, social bio

### 2. The AI tutor answers your exact question — right now.

Most content answers generic questions. Garzoni's AI tutor understands what you're studying, what you already know, and what you're confused about — and gives you a direct, practical answer at any hour.

_Where to use:_ Feature copy, paid ads, comparison content

### 3. Free to start. No excuses.

No credit card. No locked-in commitment. You can build a real 7-day streak on Starter before you decide to upgrade. The freemium model removes every barrier to starting.

_Where to use:_ CTA copy, pricing page, performance ads

## Launch Phases

### Phase 0: Foundation (Now — before any public posting)

- [x] Product marketing context finalized
- [x] Brand voice guide written
- [x] Landing and pricing copy aligned
- [ ] Set up analytics (Amplitude already integrated — define key events to track)
- [ ] Confirm signup + onboarding flow works end-to-end
- [ ] Write 3 launch posts (warm-up, announcement, feature deep-dive)

### Phase 1: Organic Soft Launch (Week 1–2)

- Post founder intro on LinkedIn and X/Twitter
- Share the "why I built this" story — the problem the product solves, the name, the Renaissance angle
- Post one feature deep-dive per week (AI tutor, streaks, tools)
- Target: 50–100 signups from personal network + organic reach

### Phase 2: Content Engine (Month 1–2)

- Publish 1 SEO article per week on personal finance topics (budgeting basics, how to start investing, etc.)
- Each article links to the relevant Garzoni learning path
- Post 3x/week on LinkedIn/X — mix of education, product, founder journey
- Target: 500 signups, measure Day-7 retention

### Phase 3: Paid Amplification (Month 2–3, post validation)

- Only after organic content proves what messaging converts
- Small-budget Meta/Google test ($10–20/day)
- Target: Starter signup → Plus conversion — validate LTV before scaling

## Channel Priorities

### Primary: LinkedIn

**Why:** UK professional audience maps directly to the ICP (entrepreneurs, professionals, career-builders). Finance education content performs well. Founder-voice content gets organic reach.

**Format:** Text posts (founder story, finance lessons), short videos (product demos, AI tutor in action), carousels (explainer content)

### Primary: X / Twitter

**Why:** Finance and edtech communities are active. Fast feedback loop. Good for teaser content and building in public.

**Format:** Short-form insights, product updates, "building in public" thread

### Secondary: SEO / Blog

**Why:** Long-term compounding channel. Personal finance has massive search volume. Articles on "how to start investing UK", "personal finance basics", "best way to learn about money" → drive Garzoni signups.

**Format:** 800–1500 word articles targeting informational queries. Each links to the relevant learning path.

### On Hold: TikTok / Instagram

**Why later:** High production effort for uncertain ROI at pre-scale. Revisit after Month 3 when messaging is validated and there's time/resource.

## Renaissance Angle Activation (Subtle)

The Garzoni name is a quiet brand story asset — use it early, don't over-explain it.

**Founder post angle:**
"The name comes from the apprentices of Renaissance Florence — the garzoni. They learned their craft through structured practice under a master. I built this platform around that same idea: financial literacy isn't a piece of content you consume. It's a skill you build, one lesson at a time."

**Where it shows up in product:**

- "Your path to mastery" in CTAs and onboarding
- Streak mechanics framed as "daily craft practice"
- Learning path progression as apprentice → practitioner → master (future feature idea)

**Where it stays subtle:**

- Never "Like the bankers of Florence..." in marketing copy
- Never medieval/archaic language
- Never Renaissance imagery in UI (unless it's very abstract — Florentine patterns as a texture, not a costume)

## Launch KPIs

| Metric                         | Definition                             | Target (Month 1) | Target (Month 3) |
| ------------------------------ | -------------------------------------- | ---------------- | ---------------- |
| Signups                        | Users completing registration          | 100              | 500              |
| Onboarding completion          | % of signups who finish onboarding     | >60%             | >70%             |
| Day-7 retention                | % of signups still active after 7 days | >25%             | >35%             |
| Streak 7+ days                 | Users with 7+ consecutive day streak   | >15% of actives  | >25% of actives  |
| Starter → Plus/Pro             | % of Starter users upgrading           | — (too early)    | >5%              |
| AI tutor prompts / active user | Engagement with AI feature             | >3 prompts/week  | >5 prompts/week  |

**What success looks like at Month 1:** 100 signups, 60%+ finish onboarding, 25%+ reach Day-7. These numbers tell you the product holds attention — which is the only thing that matters before spending on paid.

---

## What NOT to do yet

- Don't run paid ads before organic proves what messaging works
- Don't build a referral program yet (no users to refer)
- Don't post on 5 channels — pick 2 and own them
- Don't write 10 blog posts before 1 converts a single user
